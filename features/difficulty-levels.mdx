## Overview

WPM Typing Tutor offers three distinct difficulty levels to accommodate players of all skill levels. Each level adjusts both the **velocity** of text scrolling and the **complexity** of the typing content to provide an appropriate challenge.

## Difficulty Breakdown

### Fácil (Easy)

The easy difficulty mode is designed for beginners who are just starting to learn touch typing or want to practice proper finger positioning on the home row.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Text Characteristics">
    - **Simple vocabulary**: Common words and short phrases
    - **Basic punctuation**: Minimal use of special characters
    - **Shorter sentences**: Easy-to-read text fragments
    - **Repetitive patterns**: Helps build muscle memory
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Speed Settings">
    - **Slower scroll velocity**: More time to locate keys
    - **Generous timing**: Reduced pressure on completion
    - **Forgiving error tolerance**: Focus on accuracy over speed
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Ideal For">
    - Players new to touch typing
    - Learning home row positioning (F and J keys)
    - Building confidence and accuracy
    - Students in early typing lessons
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  Start with Easy mode to master the home row keys (F and J for index fingers) before progressing to higher difficulties.
</Tip>

### Medio (Medium)

Medium difficulty provides a balanced challenge for typists who have mastered the basics and are ready to improve their speed while maintaining accuracy.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Text Characteristics">
    - **Varied vocabulary**: Mix of common and moderately complex words
    - **Standard punctuation**: Includes commas, periods, and basic symbols
    - **Longer sentences**: Complete thoughts and phrases
    - **Natural language flow**: More realistic typing scenarios
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Speed Settings">
    - **Moderate scroll velocity**: Requires steady typing rhythm
    - **Balanced timing**: Fair challenge without overwhelming
    - **Standard error penalties**: Errors impact final WPM score
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Ideal For">
    - Intermediate typists (40-70 WPM)
    - Practicing speed while maintaining accuracy
    - Transitioning from learning to proficiency
    - Daily typing practice sessions
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Difícil (Hard)

Hard difficulty is designed for expert typists aiming to achieve top leaderboard positions and push their typing speed to the limit.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Text Characteristics">
    - **Complex vocabulary**: Challenging words and technical terms
    - **Full punctuation**: All special characters and symbols
    - **Longer paragraphs**: Extended typing sequences
    - **Unpredictable patterns**: Tests true typing mastery
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Speed Settings">
    - **Maximum scroll velocity**: Demands rapid, accurate typing
    - **Tight timing**: High pressure environment
    - **Strict error penalties**: Mistakes significantly reduce WPM
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Ideal For">
    - Advanced typists (80+ WPM)
    - Competing for leaderboard rankings
    - Achieving personal records
    - Professional typing speed goals
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  Hard mode error penalties are severe. Each mistake significantly impacts your final WPM score. Focus on accuracy first, then speed.
</Warning>

## How to Choose Your Level

Select your difficulty level based on your current typing ability and goals:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Assess Your Skills">
    Take a moment to consider your current typing speed and accuracy. If you're unsure, start with **Easy** mode.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Difficulty">
    Choose your level from the game menu:
    ```bash
    start_game --difficulty easy
    start_game --difficulty medio  
    start_game --difficulty difícil
    ```
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Practice and Progress">
    Master each level before moving up. Aim for 95%+ accuracy before increasing difficulty.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Progression Strategy

Follow this recommended progression path to maximize your improvement:

<Note>
  **Pro Tip**: Don't rush to higher difficulties. Building proper technique at lower speeds prevents bad habits that are hard to break later.
</Note>

| Your WPM | Recommended Level | Focus Area |
|----------|-------------------|------------|
| 0-40 WPM | Fácil (Easy) | Learn home row, build muscle memory |
| 40-70 WPM | Medio (Medium) | Increase speed, maintain accuracy |
| 70-100 WPM | Medio → Difícil | Transition to advanced patterns |
| 100+ WPM | Difícil (Hard) | Compete for records, perfect technique |

## Game Controls

Controls remain consistent across all difficulty levels:

- <kbd>SPACE</kbd> - Start the game
- <kbd>ESC</kbd> - Return to menu
- <kbd>F</kbd> + <kbd>J</kbd> - Home row positioning for index fingers
